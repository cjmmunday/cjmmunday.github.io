Hi, I'm
Chris.

I’m a videographer and editor from the south west! Whether its short films, documentaries or just shooting B-roll for the fun of it... every project comes from the same place: a genuine love of what film and media can do to a person, in the space of a few minutes or a few hours. That's what I’m chasing on every shoot. The strongest visuals aren’t the loudest ones... They’re the ones that serve the story and stay with someone long after the film’s finished. That belief comes from years of being the person who notices a cut, a lighting choice, a needle-drop, and can’t stop thinking about why it worked.

The details are where it actually happens – light, composition, movement, pacing. I work closely with clients to get under the surface of an idea, so the result feels distinctive, but still unmistakably theirs.

When I’m not behind the camera, I’m at a gig, making music, or deep into whatever’s next on the watchlist. I’m always studying the craft, because the goal has never just been to work in this industry. It's to help shape what it becomes.
